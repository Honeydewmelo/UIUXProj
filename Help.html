About Fortnite:

What are Rarities?

Rarities are the main grading system in Fortnite. Legendary weapons are some of the best (there are better) while Common are the worst. The rarities and their meanings are as follows:

Common

The worst weapon grade. Easiest to find
Uncommon

Second worst weapon grade. Slightly harder to find
Rare

Middle weapon grade from the classic rarities. Harder to find than Uncommon
Epic

Second best weapon grade from the classic rarities. Harder to find than Rare
Legendary

The best weapon grade from the classic rarities. The hardest to find from them
Mythic

Better than Legendary. These are mainly found by completing special events such as defeating bosses, but some may also be bought from NPCs
Exotic

These are weapons that are almost exclusively bought from NPCs. These weapons usually have better stats than a Legendary, and come with unique effects such as granting an effect on use, reload, or hit.

What do the stats mean?

The stats of a weapon are the main way to determine how good it is. The main weapon stats are as follows:

Damage

How much damage the weapon does per shot
Fire Rate

How many shots the weapon can fire per second
Reload Speed

How long it takes to reload the weapon
Magazine Size

How many shots the weapon can fire before needing to reload

What are the different types of weapons?

There are 10 different types of weapons in Fortnite. These types are as follows:

Assault Rifles

Normally use medium bullets, good at medium range and deal decent damage
Shotguns

Use shells as ammo, Most shoot a spread of pellets for big damage. Excel in short-range combat
Submachine Guns

Use light bullets as ammo, fast fire rate with low damage, best at short ranges
Pistols

Most use light bullets as ammo, good at short-medium range, and have the widest variety of stat-distribution
Sniper Rifles

Use Heavy bullets as ammo, Excel at long range due to their scopes. Bullets have travel time
Bows

Use arrows as ammo, similar to snipers, but they charge to increase the range and speed of arrows. most bows have effects when fully charged
Explosive Weapons

Use rockets as ammo, high damage with splash damage to hit enemies near the explosion.
Marksman Rifles

Use medium bullets as ammo, good at medium-long range, have a scope, and bullets have travel time
Crossbows

Usually have infinite ammo, similar to snipers, but they shoot faster, deal less damage, and their shots travel slower
Melee Weapons

Deal damage by hitting enemies with the weapon. They normally have a mobility-based secondary attack
